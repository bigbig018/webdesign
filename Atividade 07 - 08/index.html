Pokémon Types

Pokémon Types

At the very heart of the Pokémon world lies a fundamental system that governs every battle, strategy, and team composition: the Pokémon types. Much like elements in nature, these types are elemental attributes assigned to every Pokémon and their moves. This system creates a complex and strategic web of interactions, turning battles into more than just a contest of strength, but a dynamic puzzle of strengths and weaknesses. From the iconic trio of Grass, Fire, and Water to the more mysterious Dark and ethereal Fairy types, understanding these classifications is the first and most crucial step for any aspiring Pokémon Trainer.
